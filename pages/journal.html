just some thoughts

Thanks to the gentleman who screamed from his window for me to turn on my lights, when I was too naïve to realize that (1) it was quite dark outside and (2) my car was not quite tech enough to automagically activate its headlights.

And to whoever put up with my lights being off the week(s) before that.

Oh, wait. I should also include the person who decided that automagically did not deserve to be underlined by the spelling corrector.

Posted 10 November, 2020

Many thanks to those strangers who allowed me to cut to the front of the crowded ticket queue last summer to catch my northbound train.

And the friend who rushed a second trek to the airport and back to retrieve my misplaced passport.

And the plane that delayed takeoff so I could scamper on.

Yes.

Thanks to the back-havers.
