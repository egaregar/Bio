a bit about me

Curiosity killed the cat.

But it hasn't killed me yet.

From a young age, I have been that inquisitive kid who actually likes doing homework. Learning propels me, especially when it enables me to draw connections and solve problems. I am especially familiar with financial analysis, Asian tongues, and coding wizardry, and I plan to grow that list at least until I am dead.

I am markedly dependable and thrive in dynamic environments. I welcome challenges of unanswered questions and depend on data to glean insights. I value genuine relationships and embrace collaborative work.

Don't believe me? Let's connect and I will prove you wrong (in the friendliest of ways, of course).
